myTeam.py Implementation Mechanics

Global Search Utilities

  • SearchProblem (Class): An abstract interface defining the requirements for any state-space search. It forces subclasses to implement getStartState(), isGoalState(), and getSuccessors() to ensure compatibility with search algorithms.
  • aStarSearch: A generic implementation of the A* algorithm.
    How: It uses a PriorityQueue to explore states based on $f(n) = g(n) + h(n)$, where $g(n)$ is the path cost and $h(n)$ is the heuristic. It tracks best_g to ensure only the cheapest path to any state is expanded, effectively preventing redundant work in cycles.

Class: PositionProblem

Goal: Transform the Pacman grid into a formal search problem for A*.

How: By mapping (x, y) coordinates to states and calculating successor costs based on wall collisions and a dynamic danger_fn cost modifier.

Class: BeliefTracker

Goal: Maintain a real-time estimate of where hidden opponents are located.

How: By mapping sonar distance measurements to a grid of probabilities and iteratively updating them as time passes and new observations arrive.

  • _noise_prior: Calculates the likelihood of a noisy reading given a true distance by defining a uniform distribution over the sonar noise range.
  • observe: Updates belief grids by multiplying previous probabilities with new observation data, zeroing out squares that are definitely not the opponent, and re-normalizing the sum to 1.
  • elapseTime: Spreads probability mass from each cell to its adjacent legal neighbors, effectively "blurring" the agent's knowledge to account for the opponent moving one square in any direction.
  • danger_grid: Computes a heatmap by summing the probability of every non-scared ghost across all positions, weighted by their current belief distribution.

Class: BaseCaptureAgent

Goal: Provide a unified framework for pathfinding and decision-making.

How: By centralizing shared map data (walls, boundaries) and standardizing the communication between the tactical logic and the search algorithms.

  • buildDangerFn: Returns a closure that maps a position to a cost by checking the danger_grid and applying a neighborhood penalty to account for proximity to ghosts.
  • planTo: Initiates a search by creating a PositionProblem instance that combines standard grid traversal with the danger_fn cost modifier.
  • pickSafestLegal: Scans all valid immediate moves and ranks them by their danger cost, selecting the lowest-cost action to avoid immediate collision.

Class: OffenseAgent

Goal: Maximize food collection while minimizing risk of capture.

How: By dynamically switching between goal-seeking (food) and survival (fleeing) based on internal state triggers like current load, ghost proximity, and proximity to capsules.

  • _ghostAdjacent: Evaluates risk by querying the BeliefTracker for the most likely position of any non-scared ghost and checking the maze distance against a fixed radius.

Class: DefenseAgent

Goal: Patrol boundaries and intercept intruders.

How: By prioritizing the interception of detected invaders, or, in the absence of sightings, patrolling locations that provide the best coverage of the home territory.

  • _keepDistance: Forces the agent to oscillate or hover at a specific maze distance from an intruder, preventing the agent from over-committing to a chase if it is currently "scared".
